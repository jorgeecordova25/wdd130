Song of the week

Twenty One Pilots
Twenty One Pilots is an American musical duo known for their eclectic style blending elements of pop, rock, hip-hop, and electronica. Their lyrics often delve into emotional and personal themes, capturing a broad audience with their distinctive and energetic sound.
ABBA
ABBA was a Swedish musical group active in the 1970s and early 1980s. Known for their catchy pop music and infectious melodies, ABBA is one of the most successful groups in history, with hits like "Dancing Queen," "Mamma Mia," and "Waterloo." Their distinctive style and vocal harmonies have made them an icon of pop music.
Surf Curse
Surf Curse is an American indie rock band known for their lo-fi, surf-inspired sound. Formed in 2013, the duo creates music characterized by fuzzy guitars, catchy melodies, and introspective lyrics. With songs like "Heathers" and "Forever Dumb," Surf Curse has garnered a dedicated following within the indie music scene.
